Crowne Plaza Hotel Beijing is located in Tianzhu Airport Economic Development Zone, and the New International Exhibition Center Zhichizhiyao, from the Capital International Airport only 15 minutes. The hotel is adjacent to Beijing's largest luxury villa residential area, with a variety of international restaurants around.
