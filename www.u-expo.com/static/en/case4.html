• About us

    China United Expo Service Co., Ltd was established in August 2008. It is an exhibition company engaged in organizing various types of domestic and international exhibitions services, as well as planning business meetings.

    We not only provided a range of professional services, but also have established a wide network of contacts and good cooperative relations with the relevant government departments and industry leading departments.

    Our company has successfully undertaken the National Agricultural Exhibition Center projects, which fully shows our strength and advantages in the exhibition industry.

    After years of unremitting efforts, we now have a considerable scale exhibition production factory, a professional construction team and skilled workers.

    We offer our customers with one-stop service, and also ensure that enterprise customers get the most reasonable price, the most reliable quality assurance.

    In the future, with first-class service, rich experience, sharp market sense and understanding of customer needs, the team will continue to provide our new and old customers with more professional, high-quality, thoughtful and diversified service.
